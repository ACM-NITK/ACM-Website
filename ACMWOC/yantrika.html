What is Yantrika?

Yantrika is a special interest group of ACM-NITK student chapter dedicated to engineering applications in the field of Mechanical, Civil, Chemical and Material Science.

What do we do at Yantrika?

Through multiple events, KEPs and Projects, Yantrika aims to bring out the technical aspect of engineering in everyone. Be it a beginner or a tech-wizard, Yantrika provides a platform to always learn more!

Our motivation

Yantrika allows its members to harness knowledge and to apply it in practical situations. In the ever-diverse field of engineering, we help people focus on their area of specialization and to explore new areas of interests, be it related to any branch of engineering. Having collaborative projects with other SIGs, Yantrika is the ultimate tech hub for all the budding engineers out there!

Events

Jugaad

Theory, Design, Analysis, manufacturing and every other technical segment of core engineering you can think of will be put to test in this week-long event as participants go head to head in this ultimate technical event. Each round tests a different ability, and with each task the participants learn to create, improvise, and of course, to jugaad their way through as they race against the clock.
